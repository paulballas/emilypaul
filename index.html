Our Story

How it all started

Emily and Paul both attended Radford University and lived in Arlington, VA after college, but didn’t meet each other until years later… despite having numerous mutual friends. It was not until a friend’s birthday celebration in 2012, where fate brought them together. They instantly hit it off and have been together ever since.

The couple knew this was the real deal and decided to move in together a year later. Paul proposed at the Meadowlark Botanical Gardens shortly after moving in together.  The happy couple currently lives in their favorite DC neighborhood, Bloomingdale, in Washington, DC. They enjoy traveling, exercising and biking together.

our many adventures

Emily and Paul love to travel, and have visited numerous cities together for both work and play.

San Francisco - California

Both working in the tech industry, the couple loves visiting Silicon Valley and touring their favorite companies. While there, they went hiking in Muir Woods, explored downtown San Francisco and had Emily's birthday dinner Chez Panisse in Berkley.

Montreal - Canada

Emily went to Montreal for a week for a work conference, and Paul tagged along to explore the city with her. They went to a variety of restaurants, will working on their French. Poutine is better than it looks.

Boulder - Colorado

Known as Paul's favorite city, the couple spent a week hiking and biking in the Rocky Mountains. They climbed to over 10,000 feet and visited a different brewery everday. They may or may not have visited one of marijuana dispensaries.
