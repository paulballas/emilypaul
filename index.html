Our Story

How it all started

Emily and Paul's paths have to have crossed while attending Radford University and living in Arlington, Virginia at the same time, however, the couple did not meet until years after college. It was at a mutual friend’s birthday celebration in 2012, when fate brought them together. They instantly hit it off and have been together ever since.

Paul proposed at the Meadowlark Botanical Gardens shortly after moving in together.  The happy couple currently lives in their favorite Washington, DC neighborhood, Bloomingdale. They enjoy traveling, exercising, being foodies and biking together.

The Couple That Travels Together Stays Together

Emily and Paul love to travel, and have visited numerous cities together for both work and play.

San Francisco - California

Both working in the tech industry, the couple loves visiting Silicon Valley and touring their favorite companies. While on vacation they went hiking in Muir Woods and explored downtown San Francisco.

Montreal - Canada

Emily traveled to Montreal for a work conference. Paul tagged along to explore the city with her. They went to a variety of restaurants, worked on their French, and played with a living art exhibit, a swinging piano organ. The poutine was also better than it looked!

Boulder - Colorado

Known as Paul's favorite city, the couple spent a week hiking and biking in the Rocky Mountains. They climbed to over 10,000 feet and visited a different brewery everday. They also enjoyed a free concert with local beer, bands and hoola-hoop dancers.
